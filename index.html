Nuclear Throne is a top-down roguelite game developed by Vlambeer studios and releasted in 2015. It is fast-paced for the genre and features a wide selection of enemies, characters, and weapons to use. On this wiki, you can find some Tips and Tricks for your runs, how to get to Secret Levels, and a list of all the characters and their various abilities. It's currently available on PC, Mac & Linux, PlayStation 4, and PlayStation Vita. It will later launch on PlayStation 3 and Xbox One. Purchase the game for PC on Steam or the Game's Website.
