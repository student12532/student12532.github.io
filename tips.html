Tips & Tricks

Mutations

The game features a unique and interesting "mutation" system. Every time you level up (by collecting rads) you can choose a new ability to gain. These include (but are not limited to):

  • Rhino skin (+4 max health).
  • Long arms (extra range on melee weapons).
  • Open mind (extra chest spawn every level).
  • Throne Butt (characters special ability gets stronger).
  • Extra feet (increased movement speed).
  • Scary face (less enemy HP).

Tips

  • Choose your mutations carefully depending on which character you are playing.
  • Use cover to your advantage to avoid getting hit.
  • Health is your most valuable resource.
  • Use your characters abilities to your advantage.
  • Practice!
  • Don't be afraid to play it safe.
